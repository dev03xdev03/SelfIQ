2. Scope of Data Processing

2.1 Guest Mode:

• Device ID (anonymous identifier of your smartphone)
• Self-chosen guest name (no real names required)

• Guest account creation timestamp
• Completed tests and their results
• Last activity (for 72h time limitation)

This data is stored exclusively locally on your device and in our database, but NOT linked to your personal identity.

2.2 Apple Sign-In:

• Apple ID (encrypted)
• Email provided by Apple (optionally anonymized)
• Username
• Gender (f/m/d) - automatically detected
• Device ID for synchronization
• All test results and progress
• Account creation date and last activity

2.3 Technical Data:

• Device type and operating system version
• App version
• Error logs (crash reports)
• Usage statistics (anonymized)

The processing of your data is based on:

• Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract fulfillment) - to provide app functionality
• Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests) - to improve the app and analyze errors
• Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent) - for optional features like analytics
